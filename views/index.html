What is Matrioska?

Matrioska is a fast-paced word game where your goal is to find smaller, hidden words inside a main "Master Word". The longer the hidden word you find, the more points you score. Keep peeling the letters to climb the ranks!

Play with Friends!

You don't have to play alone. Join public lobbies or create private rooms to challenge your friends in real-time. Choose your favorite game mode, test your vocabulary, and see who dominates the global leaderboard!

HOW TO PLAY

1

Get the Master Word

Every round starts with a long "Master Word". Your mission is to look closely at its letters and find hidden meanings.

2

Peel the Layers

Find smaller words hidden inside the main word. Golden Rule: The letters must appear in the exact direct or indirect sequence.

3

Score Big

Submit your words before the timer runs out. The more layers you uncover, or the longer the hidden word is, the more points you earn!
